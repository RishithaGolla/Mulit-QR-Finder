Privacy Policy for Multi QR Finder

Last updated:

Multi QR Finder is a Chrome extension that scans visible QR codes on the current page and shows the decoded links so you can open, copy, or save them. This privacy policy explains what data the extension uses and how it is handled.

Data we access

When you click the “Scan QR codes on this page” button, the extension reads the images on the current tab in order to detect and decode QR codes. This processing happens locally in your browser.

Data we store

If you choose to save a decoded QR link, the extension stores:

  • The URL of the decoded link
  • The name you entered for the link (optional)
  • The date and time when it was saved

This information is stored using Chrome’s extension storage on your device so that you can view your history later. You can delete individual items or clear the entire history at any time from within the extension.

Data we do not collect

Multi QR Finder does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information
  • Passwords, authentication data, or payment information
  • Browsing history across sites
  • Analytics, tracking identifiers, or advertising data

The extension does not send any data to external servers or third parties. All QR detection and decoding are performed locally in your browser.

Third parties

Multi QR Finder does not share any data with third parties and does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Your choices

You can:

  • Use the extension without saving any links to history
  • Delete individual saved items
  • Clear all saved history from the extension
  • Uninstall the extension at any time from your browser
